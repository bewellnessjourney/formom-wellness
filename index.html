Prenatal Treatment

Bali Reeva Spa
Prenatal Massage

Balinese Traditional Technique · 60 or 90 mins

Drawing from the ancient healing traditions of Bali, this specialised prenatal massage is thoughtfully adapted for expectant mothers. Using safe, nurturing techniques, our practitioners ease the physical discomforts of pregnancy while restoring deep calm to body and mind.

  • Relieves lower back pain, hip tension and muscle fatigue
  • Reduces leg and foot swelling through gentle lymphatic stimulation
  • Calms the nervous system and improves sleep quality
  • Promotes healthy circulation for mother and baby
  • Eases anxiety and nurtures emotional wellbeing

Available from the second trimester onwards. All treatments are adapted to your pregnancy stage by trained practitioners.

Traditional Malay · Door-to-Door · 10 Days

Bengkung ForMom

An ancient Malay tradition of wrapping the postpartum abdomen with high-quality cotton cloth — creating gentle, even compression to support healing, restore posture, and strengthen the core. Our expert practitioners bring this transformative ritual directly to your home for 10 consecutive days.

Deposit
